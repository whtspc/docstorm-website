DocStorm — Business Value Propositions

1

Speed Drives Adoption, Adoption Drives Data Quality

Minutes of manual entry become seconds. That speed difference matters—not just for efficiency, but because friction kills adoption. When entering data is effortless, people actually do it. Your CRM fills up with reliable, actionable information instead of empty records.

2

One Tool, Unlimited Document Types

Because DocStorm is built on LLM technology rather than rigid OCR templates, you're not locked into predefined document categories. Invoices today, contracts tomorrow, handwritten field inspection reports next week—whatever your business throws at it. The same tool adapts to your imagination, not the other way around.

3

Intelligence Beyond Extraction

This isn't just about pulling data off a page. The LLM foundation means DocStorm can summarize lengthy documents, surface action items, and add contextual understanding that traditional OCR simply can't deliver. You're not just digitizing documents—you're making them actionable.

4

Zero Integration Overhead, Enterprise-Grade Trust

Built natively on Agentforce means no third-party vendors, no external data flows, no authentication complexity to maintain. Your data stays within Salesforce's security perimeter—backed by decades of cloud security expertise and their foundational commitment to trust.
